What is Home Isles?

Home Isles was a life sim or "cozy" game much like animal crossing released for the Playstation Portable.
It was developed by the up and coming game development company "Fo-Fune."
Home Isles had many every day activities, such as: Fishing, Logging, farming, and a few other activities.
It was also notable for apparently having a very adaptive character system, that could form sentences based off of user
input. Despite its promising content, it sadly sold relatively poorly in Japan and the US.
The most likely reason was probably the fact it had an apparently extremely rushed development
Thus leading to a lack of information surrounding game.
